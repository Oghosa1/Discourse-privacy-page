Privacy Policy for PolyLogue AI

Effective date: January 2026

PolyLogue AI is a real-time AI group chat application. This policy explains how we handle your data when you interact with our AI personas and other users.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: We collect your email address and name to manage your account and "Energy" credits.
  • Chat Content: We process the text you send in chats so our AI agents can respond. This data may be briefly stored to provide conversation context.
  • Financial Data: We use RevenueCat to handle subscriptions. We see your purchase history but do not store your credit card details.

2. Third-Party AI Processors

PolyLogue AI utilizes the Gemini API to provide chat functionality. Data sent to Gemini includes your chat prompts and user inputs. We do not share your email, name, or location with Gemini. Gemini is committed to data protection and does not use API data to train their models (per their Enterprise/API terms: Gemini API Additional Terms of Service; Generative AI Prohibited Use Policy).

3. Data Retention & Deletion

We store your data securely using encrypted backend services. You can request full deletion of your account and all associated chat history at any time through our Data Deletion Page.
